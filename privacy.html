Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Futof Media House, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our games, use our apps, or visit our website.

We may collect:

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our games, apps, and services
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance

Third-Party Services

Our games and apps may include third-party services such as Google Play Services, Firebase Analytics, or AdMob. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services

Children’s Privacy

Our games and apps are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Data Retention & Security

We only retain data as long as necessary to provide our services and take reasonable steps to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal data, or opt out of analytics/advertising tracking where applicable. To exercise these rights, contact us using the form on our contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new “Effective Date.”
